Lee Felix


Lee Felix (이필릭스) was born as Felix Lee on September 15, 2000 in Sydney, Australia. He is the lead rapper and lead dancer of the boy group Stray Kids under JYP Entertainment. Aside from rapping, he also has a knack for beatboxing and playing the piano. Felix is best recognized for his distinct deep voice which is showcased in Stray Kids' songs. Felix debuted as a member of Stray Kids on March 25, 2018 with "I am NOT" and its title track "District 9."
